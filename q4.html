How does education impact migration rate?

Question info

  • x-axis: mean years of schooling for a country's population
  • y-axis: mean migration rates for a country
  • time period: this data represents the range 2015-2020

This visualization focuses on how education and time spent in school affects the migration rates for certain countries. Along the x axis is the mean years each country's population has spent in school, and along the y axis is the migration rate for our selected year range (2015-2020). This data proposes a correlation between education and migration rates, as there tends to be more spikes as you move right across the x axis. Countrynames are kept anonymous here, to provide an unbiased look at how the two attributes correspond.
