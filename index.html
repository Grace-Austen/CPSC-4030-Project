School Rules, Actually

This is our Data Visualization project, utilizing d3.js to express the data openly provided to us from the Wittgenstein Centre for Demography and Global Human Capital.
We wanted to use this public data to answer the question of whether or not education impacts opportunity, specifically tied to migration and fertility possibilities.
